About Us

Ridesharing already existed, but offers a whole new way to share your trips and travel costs.
An economical alternative to traditional carpooling services, Fi-Thneyti allows you to carpool with people who are already going in the same direction as you. Are you a driver? Submit the free spaces for your car on Fi-Thneyti app. Are you a passenger? Use Fi-Thneyti Carpool app to find drivers on your route.
